Adopt a Shelter Cat or Dog

All of our pets are spayed/neutered (kapon) and vaccinated. Since many have come from difficult lives, we want to ensure they find safe, loving homes. Here’s how to adopt:

  • 📄 Submit the adoption application
  • 🎥 Attend the Zoom interview
  • 🐾 Meet our shelter animals in person
  • 💛 Visit and confirm your chosen pet
  • 🩺 Wait for vet clearance
  • 💵 Pay adoption fee: ₱500 (cat) / ₱1000 (dog)
  • 🏡 Take your new best friend home!
